### Formulation of Business Problems:

By utilizing data-driven insights, we hope to improve energy efficiency and lower expenses for individual families. Our goal is to create a predictive model that, using past data, can estimate a household's future power usage using the "Individual Household Electric Power Consumption" dataset. The main goal is to provide households with the knowledge and skills necessary to monitor and optimize their electricity use by enabling them to make educated decisions about how much energy they consume. Our aim is to empower consumers to recognize possible periods of excessive energy consumption, comprehend energy usage patterns, and execute tactics to reduce electricity costs by offering precise forecasts.
